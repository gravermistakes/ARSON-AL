# Web3 Bug Bounty Platforms

## Immunefi (`immunefi.com`)

- **Focus**: The leading Web3 bug bounty platform. Focuses on smart contracts, DeFi, and blockchain infrastructure.
- **Triage**: Has a structured, rule-bound triage system with high severity payouts. Runs the Immunefi Vulnerability Severity Classification System.

---

## Code4rena (`code4rena.com`)

- **Focus**: Crowdsourced competitive smart contract security audits. "Arena" style where auditors compete to find bugs within limited-time contests.
- **Triage**: Triage is done by community judges, grouping duplicates and grading severity (L/M/H).

---

## Sherlock (`sherlock.xyz`)

- **Focus**: Web3 security platform combining audits, bug bounties, and smart contract coverage.
- **Triage**: Includes dedicated lead senior watson reviews and judges to moderate disputes.

---

## HackenProof (`hackenproof.com`)

- **Focus**: Hybrid platform managing both Web2 and Web3 programs. Includes crowdsourced bug bounty and penetration testing.

---

## Spearbit (`spearbit.com`)

- **Focus**: Security consulting network delivering high-quality enterprise smart contract reviews and structured training programs.

---

## Hats Finance (`hats.finance`)

- **Focus**: Decentralized on-chain bug bounty vaults where tokens are deposited as incentive, and payouts are handled programmatically through smart contract triggers.
