# Web3 Threat Intelligence Feeds

## Postmortem and Incident Analysis Blogs

- **Immunefi Medium**: Detailed walk-throughs of major Web3 exploits and incident postmortems.
- **BlockSec Blog**: High-fidelity technical summaries of ongoing/recent exploits, including execution traces and specific root causes.
- **CertiK / Beosin / Neptune Mutual Blogs**: Incident databases and post-incident reports.
- **web3sec.news**: Consolidated repository of Web3 security updates, papers, and tools.

---

## Newsletters and Threat Intelligence Feeds

- **Blockchain Threat Intelligence**: Newsletter compiling weekly developments in on-chain threats, wallet drains, smart contract vulnerabilities, and regulatory updates.
- **REKT** (`rekt.news`): Dark-humor and highly technical autopsy reports of decentralized finance exploits, including leaderboards of the largest hacks.
- **Week in Ethereum News**: Broad development updates containing contract security and compiler warning segments.
- **HashingBits**: Weekly security newsletter with deep dives into specific smart contract vulnerabilities and auditing methodologies.
